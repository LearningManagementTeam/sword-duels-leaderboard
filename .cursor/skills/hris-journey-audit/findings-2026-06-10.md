## Executive summary

HRIS is correctly positioned as **upstream org data** (135 branches, employee profiles). Recent wins: wide profile drawer, in-directory rep assignment, Gemini bulk screenshot import. Main tension: **two rep-assignment mental models** (employee-centric vs branch-centric) without a unified readiness dashboard.
